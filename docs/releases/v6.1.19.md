# v6.1.19

Release date: 2026-07-03

## Summary

This patch release refines the full-window Sun and Moon cards so each keeps a clearer visual identity: a softer low dawn hint for the Sun, and a restored light-blue halo with tighter border control for the Moon.

## Highlights

- Reduced the Sun card’s lower dawn shape so it reads as a subtle sunrise remnant rather than a dominant arc.
- Kept most of the Sun field in daytime sky tones.
- Restored the Moon card’s `light_blue` halo.
- Kept `light_blue` out of the Moon’s broader background while limiting `light_gray` to the one-cell outer border.

## Included Updates

- Updated Sun and Moon full-window motif definitions in `features/project-core/motifs/single-motifs.js`.
- Updated release metadata in `package.json`, `index.html`, `CHANGELOG.md`, `README.md`, and `docs/releases/README.md`.

## Accessibility Compliance
